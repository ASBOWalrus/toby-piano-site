About

Toby is an aspiring young pianist looking for opportunities to spread his love of music to anyone who shares that love. He has a diverse repertoire from love songs to Disney classics. The music Toby plays is always familiar to the listener. A pianist’s repertoire is always expanding, and Toby is therefore happy to learn any pieces that you require.

As a child Toby was a committed member of the local church choir, and has now played the piano for 14 years. His parents’ emphasis on musicianship means that Toby can not only play the notes on the sheets, he can make music of it. During his teenage years, he was awarded his Grade 8 in Piano. More recently he has accompanied choir rehearsals and performed a classical set for Allegro Appassianato in Louth.
